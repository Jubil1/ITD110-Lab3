About this system

This is an ITD110 Lab 3 demo app: a small management UI on top of a Neo4j graph database. The backend (Node.js + Express) stores Student, Faculty, and Course data as nodes and connects them with relationships— not with foreign keys in a single flat table.

  • Students can be linked to courses through an ENROLLED_IN relationship.
  • Faculty can be linked to courses through a TEACHES relationship.
  • Courses are the hub: the course list shows who is enrolled and who is teaching, pulled from the graph.

Tip: Add a few courses first, then assign students and faculty so the dropdowns and tables stay in sync.
